The Surf Club is catered for the people, by the people. When you walk through our doors, you are part of a family. We take pride in our community and borough and provide you with the type of downt-to-earth atmosphere and warmth of service that is emblematic of the Surf Town of Rockaway Beach, Queens, NY. Cheers!
